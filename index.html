Key Eras in History

1899

Founding: Joan Gamper leads the first meeting at Solé Gym. The colors blue and garnet are chosen.

1974

Cruyff Era: Johan Cruyff joins as a player, later returning as coach to create the 'Dream Team'.

2009

The Sextuple: Under Pep Guardiola, Barça becomes the first club ever to win 6 trophies in one year.

2015

MSN Era: Messi, Suárez, and Neymar lead the club to its second historic Treble.
